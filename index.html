This game is based on your general culture, it consists of two players so to win it you have to score more then your opponent!!

How does the game work??

  • On your turn click on the start button to spin the wheel
  • Once the wheel has stopped in a specific category, a question will pop up with two choices
  • Choose the answer by clicking on the choice button
  • If you guessed the right answer your score will be increased by five points
  • Time for your opponent to play !!
